# End-User Manual (Student & Faculty)

This manual guides end-users (Students & Faculty) navigating their role workspaces.

---

## 🎓 Student Portal Workspace

### 1. Navigating your Dashboard
Log in as a Student to automatically view your personal command layout:
*   **KPI Metrics**: Tracks your current class attendance percentage (with warnings if below 75%), CGPA projections, and due fees.
*   **Today's Lectures**: Timetable schedules listing room numbers, teachers, and timings.

### 2. Utilizing the AI Study Assistant
Click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner:
*   Select **Doubt Solver** and type academic questions (e.g. *"Explain Big-O complexity"*) for instant explanations.
*   Select **AI Study Planner** to generate exam prep templates.

---

## 🍎 Faculty Portal Workspace

### 1. Classroom Operations
Log in as a Faculty to load the instruction panel:
*   **Engagement Heatmaps**: Renders class contribution grids.
*   **Student Alerts**: Highlights students flagged with attendance shortages.

### 2. Faculty AI Copilot
Use the AI workspace tools on the right:
*   **Question Paper Generator**: Enter topics to draft exam questions.
*   **Notice Dispatch**: Compile parent alert circulars.
